Feature Descriptions

Check Score: This feature allows you to search for and view detailed exam results by entering a valid registration number.

Score Report: This feature generates a report categorizing students' scores into four levels: Excellent (>=8 points), Good (6-8 points), Average (4-6 points), and Weak (<4 points).

Top 10 Students: This feature lists the top 10 performing students in Group A subjects (Math, Physics, Chemistry) ranked by total scores.
